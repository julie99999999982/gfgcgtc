About ALCU 2025

The ALCU X GAMES 2025 is an annual multi-sports and cultural event organized by the Association of Local Colleges and Universities (ALCU) in the Philippines. It serves as a platform for student-athletes and artists from various Local Universities and Colleges (LUCs) to showcase their talents, foster camaraderie, and promote holistic development. It was hosted by Tubod College (TC) in Tubod, Lanao del Norte.

The ALCU Experience

ALCU (Association of Local Colleges and Universities) is a prestigious organization that brings together students from various local institutions to foster leadership, academic excellence, and community engagement. The 2025 gathering united over 500 participants from 120 colleges nationwide for collaborative learning and networking.
